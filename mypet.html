Robert the cat

He is a cat!

I adopted him from a feline rescue in California. It took him a long time to get used to his new living conditions, but he eventually came to trust me. Now, four years later, I'm still the only person he will let get anywhere near him.

Here is a list of his likes and dislikes:

  • Likes:
    • Seafood
    • Belly rubs
    • Toe biting
    • Arm licking
  • Dislikes:
    • Chicken
    • Being picked up
    • Unexpected movement
    • Other living creatures

He is gray and white.
